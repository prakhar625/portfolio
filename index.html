Hi there, thanks for visiting my site.

As a versatile Product Designer with 4 years of professional experience, my goal in every project is to reimagine and create experiences in ways that are functional, useable, unique and emotionally compelling.

My focus on designing experiences for customers as a discipline compells me to work at the higest level, no matter what is the size, scope or domain of the project. I have experience working on products in real-estate and financial domains, but eager to to explore projects in learning (cognition) space, medical or any other systems bases domain where the team is trying to rethink, or reduce the problem. I also do decent amount of freelancing/contract work.

I believe any problem can be solved through Design, if the business enables it to solve it. There are multiple facets to the Design Methodology and businesses should harnesses all of its powers for the product's benefit - leading to high growth and retention in userbase.
Design is a double-edged sword and hence should be used wisely.
